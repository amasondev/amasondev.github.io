Sketboard

Sketboard is an online multiplayer skateboard simulator produced for Unreal Engine's January Jam. The theme of this jam was "Back to Square One", and so we decided to make a game of trial and error. In 48 hours we produced a somewhat working multiplayer game with ragdoll physics and destructible objects. Hey, it was our first attempt at using Unreal's replication system; it's a miracle it worked at all!
