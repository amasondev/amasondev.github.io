Antlion: Reloaded

Antlion: Reloaded is a simple top-down game created in Unity for IAT 312. The team was tasked with creating a brand new version of an unsuccessful game called Antlion. What resulted was a fun, hectic puzzle game which succeeded in teaching players without the use of tutorials.
